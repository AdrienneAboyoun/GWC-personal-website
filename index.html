about me

Hello world! My name is Adrienne, and my pronouns are they/them. I'm a software developer and computing tutor. I'm from Brooklyn, but I went to university in St. Andrews, Scotland, where I studied Computer Science and Psychology. I'm passionate about computing education, and thrilled to be working with Girls Who Code this summer! In my free time I enjoy making music, repairing old electronics (feel free to mail me the contents of your old phone drawer), collecting vinyl records, reading, and writing short stories.
